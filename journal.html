Chapter 5: New beginning 2.0
After graduating with my BFA and working briefly as a graphic designer, I chose to keep pursuing knowledge and dive deeper into interaction design. In August 2025, I moved to San Francisco to pursue an MDes in Interaction Design at California College of the Arts. Both the city and the program pushed me to explore the intersection of technology and design more deeply. It was here that I found my path in interaction design and creative technology.
